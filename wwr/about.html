DYKES WATER RAFTING COMPANY

Dykes Company is dedicated to helping clients fully experience and enjoy the beauty of the world’s adventures. Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable outdoor experiences while promoting environmental stewardship and fostering a deeper connection to nature. Our motto, “A happy you, a happy me,” reflects our commitment to creating joy and satisfaction for every client.

History

Dykes company was started during the 20th century by a person known as Dandyke. Dan the founder was not able to establish a good place for tourists to come and have fun due to financial issues. He later went to nearby bank and asked for a loan which would help him start a hotel around the river. Using the loan he built a beautiful structure along the river and bought two boats which were too small only to accommodate two people per trip. 10 years later he repaid his loan through the business and grew by buying other advanced costumes and equipment in addition he built many more beautiful structures like the swimming pools and others. Later in the year 2020 he registered his business as a company in his government as the Dykes company.
